Place
Maluti
Jharkhand
Maluti, a village in Jharkhand, India, is famous for its cluster of terracotta temples dating back to the 17th century. These temples, originally over 100 in number, are adorned with intricate terracotta carvings depicting scenes from Hindu mythology and daily life. The village is an important heritage site, showcasing the unique architectural and cultural traditions of the region. Efforts have been made to preserve and restore these temples, highlighting Maluti's significance as a center of historical and religious importance. The village continues to attract tourists and researchers interested in India's rich cultural heritage.
